IN DE POEZENBOOT?

It is possible to go inside the boat. If you make a reservation you do not have to stand in line(this is useful as it can be very busy). Inside you can interact with the cats as much as you like. They also have merchandise, with the money they make from this they take care of the cats. You can also make a voluntary donation.
